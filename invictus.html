Meet the Team

Michal W. Kmita
Founder/Executive Director

In 2008, Michal used the skills he acquired in real estate development and finance to assist in the construction of a school and clinic in a refugee camp outside of Kabul, Afghanistan. Upon returning back to his home in Denver, he and new friend David founded The Invictus Initiative.

Drew Ferren
Director of Global Programs

Drew is a native Coloradan with two engineering degrees from Colorado School of Mines. His background in environmental and mechanical engineering has helped him hit some large milestones with his 5+ years of work in the energy sector. For the last three years, Drew has spent a great deal of time traveling around the world in search of building great relationships, helping those in need, and living the ultimate adventure.

Cody Clickner
Director of Outreach & Community Building

Since his first trip with The Invictus Initiative to Kenya, where he proposed to his then fiancé and current wife, Cody has taken the relationships first model literally and is committed to building sustainable partnerships with volunteers, college campuses, and community partners. His background is in electrical engineering and he is also passionate about Tuskers and basketball.

Sabina Muslimovic
VP of Content & Design

Sabina was born in Bosnia and also lived in Germany before settling down in the beautiful state of Colorado. She has a BA in English Literature and minor in Technology, Arts & Media from CU Boulder, and combines her love of writing and creative design skills to help tell the Invictus brand story. Sabina is passionate about exploring the importance of relationships and helping others seek out a higher calling and purpose.

Adam Lantelme
VP of Storytelling

Raised in North Carolina, Adam moved to Salt Lake City to study history at the University of Utah. After volunteering abroad in Kenya, he decided to switch gears and pursue a life worth living. Eventually honing in his skills as a copywriter and marketer, he and his amazing wife now live to serve others.

Ian Foley
VP of Strategy & Business Development

Ian grew up in Colorado and received his BA in Finance from the University of Northern Colorado and an MBA from the University of Denver. He’s worked in process improvement, automation and data technology, finance, and consulting. When he’s not spending time with his remarkable wife, daughter, and Australian Shepherd, he’s helping the Invictus Initiative with strategy and business development.

Juan Beltran
VP of Programs, Central & South America

Juan was raised in Colorado where he received his BS in Engineering from Colorado School of Mines. His civil engineering focus has allowed him to work on a number of impactful projects in land development. Since his first trip with The Invictus Initiative, Juan has continued to build many lasting life changing relationships with trip alumni and the members of partnering communities in Nicaragua and Brazil. His hobbies include soccer, indoor rock climbing, and video games.

Gladys Anyenya
VP of Programs, Africa

Gladys was born in Kenya before attending college in Michigan as a Zawadi Africa Scholar. She has a BS in Mathematics and Physics from Hillsdale College and an MS in Mechanical Engineering from Colorado School of Mines. She is currently working on a PhD in Mechanical Engineering with an emphasis on Energy Systems Optimization at CSM. Gladys is passionate about improving the lives of people in Africa through innovative energy research and working with international communities to empower young people through education mentoring programs.

Jess Ourand
Program Coordinator, Asia

Jess is originally from Virginia Beach, Virginia. He studied social sciences at Old Dominion University where he assisted in the development of a mentorship program for at-risk youth in low-income communities. He has spent the past few years living in and travelling Asia while working as an educator. He has a passion for experiential education programs and building positive relationships in Southeast Asia. Jess is a two-wheel enthusiast and loves to explore new destinations by way of motorcycle.
